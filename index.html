Test your skills, learn new concepts, and annihilate the competition.

Binary Exploitation

Binary Exploitation is the process of taking a compiled executable and making it do whatever you want it to do. Work your way up from kiddie challenges to the level of security professionals by learning how to do everything your professors can't teach you.

Reverse Engineering

Reverse engineers analyze complex programs to see how they work...and then break them. Start learning the foundations of video game hacking, key generation, and application cracking.

Scripting

Learn how to interact with remote servers, automate tedious processes, and generally make your life easier as a Computer-Person. Weather you prefer Python, Ruby, or C, knowing at least one scripting language is a must for CTF players and Sys-Admins alike.

Web-App Exploitation

Learn all of the tricks hackers use security compromise web servers, take town websites, and steal valuable information. then, learn how to protect yourself and your websites from these attacks.

Forensics

Digital forensics is a branch of forensic science encompassing the recovery and investigation of material found in digital devices. Uncover hidden files within files, recover lost data, and use investigative tools to cath criminals and get flags.
